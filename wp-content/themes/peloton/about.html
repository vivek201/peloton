ABOUT US

Peloton Capital

Pty Ltd ACN 149 540 018 (Peloton Capital) specialises in providing investment, corporate advisory, mergers and acquisitions, securities trading and wealth management solutions. These services are directed towards our corporate clients, private clients and investors via our independent financial advisors.

Our Directors and shareholders have particular strengths in corporate finance, capital raising and mergers and acquisitions particularly in the small to mid-cap sector of the Australian listed market.

Peloton Capital’s emphasis is to provide companies with services to meet their particular needs and with a view to concentrating on its strengths in corporate finance, and where required in capital raisings.

Peloton Capital also incorporates securities trading solutions to its portfolio of services where our experienced advisors can assist high net worth individual to institutional clients with securities trading, fixed interest, cash and structured portfolio management. Our advisors are aware that clients have different investment styles and risk profiles, thus can arrange preferences to suit your needs. Our advisors will look at identifying opportunities in a variety of security offerings to increase your prospects for additional growth in your portfolio.

Additionally to the general securities offering clients may have access to Initial Public Offerings (IPOs) and other capital raising which Peloton Capitals’s Corporate Finance team have introduced to the market. As these raising are exclusive to Peloton Capital and not available to the general public, these investments may have an added out performance potential to your portfolio.
